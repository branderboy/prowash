Custom Points System

Every dollar spent builds equity in your brand.

We will configure the app to award 10 points per dollar + 50 bonus points per visit. When a customer is 150 points away from a free wash, they are statistically 80% more likely to return to you over a competitor.

Digital Stamp Card

"Wash 9 times, get the 10th free."

A simple, proven retention mechanic digitized. This runs in parallel with the points system, giving your customers two simultaneous goals to chase. This visual progress bar is a powerful motivator.

When a user sees 7 out of 10 stamps, the urge to complete the card drives immediate visits.

"Objective Met: Visual progress indicators drive repeat visits."

Viral Referral Engine

Turning your happiest customers into a sales channel.

We will implement a unique referral code system for every user. Customers can share this via SMS or social media directly from the app. This lowers your Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) to effectively $0.

Referred customers convert at a 30% higher rate than cold traffic.

"Objective Met: Zero-cost customer acquisition channel."

Wash Streaks

Building the "Weekly Wash" habit.

The app tracks consecutive weeks with a wash. As a customer builds a streak, they earn bonus multipliers. This gamification is critical for shifting a monthly customer to a weekly customer.

We can also configure Sunday Push Notifications to remind users to "Keep their streak alive."

"Objective Met: Habit formation and frequency increase."

Smart Churn Protection

Automated Win-Back Campaigns.

The system constantly monitors user activity. If a regular customer hasn't visited in 14 days, the system automatically triggers a personalized incentive. If they still haven't returned by day 21, the offer escalates.

This automated safety net catches at-risk customers before they switch habits.

"Objective Met: Automated retention and churn reduction."
